Portfolio

HeartBeat

CA Advisor Support App

Advisor support application for Android and iOS has a convenient and straightforward interface which allows users to use the following instruments with ease: tax calculator, loan calculator, reminders of tax returns and payments.

The World Nomad Games Website

In September 2014, the first World Games of Nomads were held on the shores of Lake Issyk-Kul in Cholpn-Ata. Our team had the task of creating a bright and impressive wow-site about the main events of this games.

GARDER

The system is intended for inspectors of the city's water utility. It allows to inspect various objects for water quality, record violations, control the sending of notices to violators, and receive reports on previously detected violations. With the help of this system. It became much more accessible to monitor the current state of the equipment and to prevent the ingress of dirty water back into the pipes of the local water channel.

Elcard Mobile

The Interbank Processing Center (IPC) was intended to enter the market of individuals to provide online payment services. Client independently developed a back-end for the system. Attractor Software was responsible for the development of the client’s part of the mobile application on Android platform.

Digital Student Book

The purpose of the project is to create the basement for piloting of the first digital books in the Kyrgyz education system. Existing digital books don't allow users to create colorful illustrations or a structured text according to student book’s table of contents

Stom.Kg

CRM-system helps dentists work with their patients. The application was optimized for mobiles and tablets. It simplified the process of day-to-day dentists’ work that allows keeping records of patient records and doctors, as well as tracking applications, payments, and expenses.

Tengri Wallet

This project was built as the foundation for the possibility of connecting several banking systems, as well as the place through which a person can pay for a variety of services, ranging from personal use for municipal payments and Internet shops

The Map of Legal Service Providers

The interactive online map is an instrument that allows people to search for legal service providers in a certain region. The map contains a broad range of providers across Kyrgyzstan.

UniForm

The system for the Canadian government which helps for data collection, reporting tools and visualization. The system combines centralized data collection processing for the repository for social impact measurements and analytic instruments. This automates inputting data for fund recipients.
